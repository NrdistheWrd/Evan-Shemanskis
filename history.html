The History

As it was for many, my high school years were very formative. I wasn't the most popular kid but I definitely had my circle. This, as silly as it may sound, was why I felt like content creation came so naturally. I enjoyed time to myself because I wasn't constantly out and about with friends and got used to being alone but didn't mind it. While I was with friends though, they were so supportive of this insane idea I had to become a creator, something that is so sought after but few are recognized for. However, this was also the time I realized how much I enjoyed working with computers and technology. So, I decided to combine my interests and, in May of 2019, I posted my first YouTube video.

Then the 2020 pandemic hit. Like most of us, I had so much extra time on my hands. This was when I came up with the idea for the Tech in 2 series and also decided to hone in on my abilites and teach myself even more! Eventually, I realized that there are so many different skills I was using at a time like design, video editing, audio, script writing, SEO and more, that I decided to get an Associate's in Graphic Design at MCC after I graduated high school in 2021.
